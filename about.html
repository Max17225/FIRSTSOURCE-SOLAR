Engineering a
Sustainable Future

Our Vision

"To be the catalyst for Southeast Asia's transition to a net-zero economy through engineered excellence."

FIRSTSOURCE SOLAR Sdn Bhd. is a premier, integrated solar energy solutions provider at the forefront of Malaysia's clean energy transition. We combine deep technical expertise, innovative technology, and end-to-end project management to deliver reliable, sustainable, and cost-effective solar energy systems. From concept to commissioning and beyond, we serve as a single-point partner for businesses, industries, developers, and homeowners.

Our holistic approach across four core sectors ensures that every client receives a tailored solution that maximizes return on investment, enhances energy security, and supports national sustainability goals. Founded on principles of excellence and integrity, we are committed to powering a brighter, greener future for Malaysia.

Our approach combines data-driven site analysis with top-tier hardware to ensure that every watt generated is maximized. We partner with global technology leaders to bring the latest photovoltaic innovations to your roof.

Our Mission

To accelerate Malaysia's renewable energy adoption by providing comprehensive, high-performance solar solutions. We are dedicated to delivering exceptional value through expert Engineering, Procurement, Construction, and Commissioning (EPCC), reliable equipment supply, strategic consultancy, and intelligent asset optimization.

Sustainability
Commitment

We believe clean energy shouldn't come at a dirty cost. This is our binding pledge to our clients, our community, and the planet.

Responsible Supply Chain

We rigorously audit our suppliers to ensure zero forced labor and strict environmental compliance in the manufacturing of our PV modules.

End-of-Life Stewardship

We don't abandon our systems. We offer decommissioning services to ensure old panels are recycled, not dumped in landfills.

Net-Zero Operations

We practice what we preach. Our offices and vehicle fleet are transitioning to 100% renewable energy sources by 2050.
